Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How many users can OpenClaw support?

The Business tier is designed for teams of 5 to 50 users. Beyond 50 users, we recommend a dedicated infrastructure review to ensure performance and reliability at scale. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Does each team member get a separate AI context?

Yes. OpenClaw supports per-user context isolation so each person's conversation history is private, while shared knowledge bases and personas can be configured at the team level. We set this up during installation.

What happens if a team member leaves or joins?

Adding or removing users from your OpenClaw instance is straightforward. If you have a maintenance plan, we handle user management changes within one business day at no extra charge. Otherwise, we offer one-time change requests.

Can we use our existing server or cloud account?

Yes. We can deploy onto your existing VPS, a cloud instance in your AWS, GCP, or Azure account, or a dedicated server. If you need us to provision fresh infrastructure, we will guide you through the options and can handle provisioning on your behalf.
