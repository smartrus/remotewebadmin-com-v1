OpenClaw personal installation puts a private, self-hosted AI assistant on your messaging app of choice - today, not after a weekend of troubleshooting.

OpenClaw is open-source software that connects WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and iMessage to AI models including Claude, GPT-4, and Gemini. It runs on a small VPS that costs around $5 per month. The data never leaves your server. There are no per-message fees. You own the entire stack.

The catch: getting it running requires Node.js 24, a reverse proxy, SSL certificates, a process manager, and a WhatsApp Business API connection - roughly a dozen moving parts that need to work together. Most people hit a wall somewhere in that list.

We handle every step. From provisioning your server to pairing your first messaging channel, we take care of the technical complexity so you can focus on using your assistant. The typical installation takes 2–4 hours. Most clients send their first message the same day they book.

After handover, your AI assistant runs independently on your server. You message it through WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, or iMessage just like you message anyone else. If you choose our maintenance plan, we keep it updated and monitored so it stays running without any action from you.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What server do I need?

Any VPS with 1 vCPU and 1 GB RAM is sufficient for personal use. A Hetzner CX11 or DigitalOcean Basic Droplet costs around $5–6 per month. We can provision one for you as part of the setup if you do not already have a server.

Which AI model will my assistant use?

OpenClaw connects to any AI model via API - Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, or locally-run models like Llama. We configure whichever model you prefer during setup. You will need your own API key for cloud-hosted models.

What happens if OpenClaw stops working?

If you have an active maintenance plan, we respond within 4 hours to any reported issue. Without a maintenance plan, we offer one-time fix sessions billed at our standard hourly rate.

Can I add more messaging channels later?

Yes. Additional channel connections (WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, iMessage, Mattermost) can be added any time as an add-on service.
